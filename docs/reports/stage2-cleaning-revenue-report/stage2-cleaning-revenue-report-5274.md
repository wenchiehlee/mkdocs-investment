        - <span style="color: #228B22; font-weight: bold;">森林綠 (-2σlo to -1.5σlo)</span>: 成長動能不足
        - <span style="color: #32CD32; font-weight: bold;">萊姆綠 (-3σlo to -2σlo)</span>: 缺乏成長想像
